Welcome to Lekki Conservation Centre

State located: Cross River

Short Information:

The reserve area which covers a land area of 78 hectare is located on Lekki Peninsula. Lekki area was chosen to establish the demonstration site for the conservation project after three potential areas were surveyed in 1987.

Locating the conservation project on Lekki Peninsula informed the name of the project – Lekki Conservation Centre. The Centre was established by the Nigerian Conservation Foundation to protect the wildlife and mangrove forests of Nigeria's South-West coastline from the threat of urban development.

Special Attractions:

  • THE LONGEST CANOPY WALKWAY IN AFRICA:

    The 401m Canopy walkway was launched in 2015 and it is not a climb for the timid. It is an enthralling climb and it is 100% safe. The only time you are not allowed access to the bridge is when it is raining.

  • SEE THE FAMOUS GIANT TORTOISE:

    The 95-year-old turtle roam freely at it's very slow pace. This makes it possible to take selfies. You will feel the strong and powerful shell of the turtle that is if you are lucky that it comes out during the period your visit. It is a sight to behold!

  • PLAY THE GAME OF CHESS:

    You can also play Chess and other games. The chessboard is designed on a floor with 16 big pieces. It doesn’t really matter if you can play chess, it is for the fun of it.

  • EXPLORE AND TAKE A WALK AROUND THE NATURE PARK:

    You can wander and explore around the park. From Monkeys, to the beautiful Peacock, crocodiles, snakes and other wild animals, you will see all these. At the same time, you will find different species of plants.

  • HAVE A PICNIC OR ENJOY AN ALONE TIME:

    The shed made of raffia is a cool place to relax, rejuvenate, and recover your strength. At these sheds which numbers over eight, you can have a picnic or an alone time.

  • Entrance fee:

    Children between 1-10 years: #200
    Adults: #1000.
    To climb the bridge, you have to pay an extra thousand Naira in addition to the entry fee.
    Group of 30: #25,000
