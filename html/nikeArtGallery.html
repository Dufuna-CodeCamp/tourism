Welcome to Nike Art Gallery

State located: Lagos

About Nike Art Gallery:

Nike Art Gallery is an art gallery in Lagos owned by Nike Davies-Okundaye. The gallery is arguably the largest of its kind in West Africa. Housed in a five-storey tall building, it boasts a collection of about 8,000 diverse artworks from various Nigerian artists.

Special Attractions:

  • Large piece of Nigerian Art:

    The gallery shows and sells work done by Nigerian artistes and is said to be the largest gallery of its kind. The art at the gallery includes work by such artists as Ndidi Dike, Peju Alatise, Tola Wewe, Ayorinde Olotu, Kolade Oshinowo and of course, Nike herself.

  • All shades of Creativity:

    The inside of the gallery is beautiful from its unusual doors to its bright white walls covered in all sorts of art work ranging from paintings and sculptures to fabrics and mini-installations. With so many pieces of art to look at, itâ€™s easy to feel overwhelmed.

  • Indulge your eyes in beauty!:

    Some of the pieces at the gallery cost millions of naira but there are also more affordably-priced works of art there. And if buying a piece of art is simply out of the question at the moment, indulging your eyes in the beauty created by Nigerian artists is free. Go enjoy some of the most beautiful art you will see in Lagos at the Nike Art Gallery.
