Welcome to Jabi Boat Club

State located: Abuja

About Jabi Boat Club:

Jabi Boat Club (JBC), is a family entertainment center designed to offer water based recreational facilities for the people of Abuja and its environs as well as tourists, reaching out to all ages and inclinations. The state-of-the-art water-based sporting facilities makes the Jabi Lake a compelling place to visit for both families and individuals with the flair for water sports and recreation. Their customer service is top notch and there is never a dull moment while you hang out there.

Special Attractions and Treats:

  • THE BEST BOAT EXPERIENCE IN ABUJA

    You’ll love every moment spent using the boats. The state-of-the-art watercrafts will leave you amazed and already planning your next visit. They have Jet Skis, Pontoon Party Boats, Kayaks, Pedal Boats and Fishing Boats. You’ll enjoy your boating experience with them.

  • TASTY MEALS AND COCKTAILS

    The Woodhouse Restaurant is designed and prepared to satisfy your every taste. From local delicacies to continental meals, it's certain that you’ll go back for more. For some of the best cocktails and mocktails, The Woodhouse Bar is fully stocked and waiting to satisfy every thirst.

  • PARTY HARD!

    From reggae nights, celebrity stacked events, fitness parties, boat regattas, love themed parties, to kids parties – there is always delivery of a mind blowing experience.
